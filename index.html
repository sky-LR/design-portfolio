About Me

Welcome! I'm Sky LaRoche.

I'm a fourth-year UI/UX and Graphic Design student at UC Davis. My design approach combines a strong foundation in design principles with experimental flair, creating simple, readable interfaces with an artsy twist. Inspired by modernism, postmodernism, and metamodernism, I draw from diverse influences like handmade items, classic swiss typography, punk zines, and vintage electronics. Aspiring to be a UX unicorn, I bring solid HTML/CSS/JS skills to the table. Join me in a design journey that's all about innovation and breaking the mold. Check out my portfolio for a taste of creativity meeting functionality.
